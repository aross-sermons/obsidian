### Cooking Guidelines
Recommendations for how to cook certain foods, regardless of recipe.
### Chicken
**Whole Oven Baked Chicken**
- Minimum Safe Temperature: 165f
- Bake at 375-400f for 45min-2hours
- Larger chickens cook lower and slower
